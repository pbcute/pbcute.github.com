About Us





Welcome to our motorcycle rental in Siargao Island, Philippines! We are a locally-owned and operated business that has been serving tourists and locals in the island. Goyjek offers premium scooter rentals in Siargao Island, Philippines. Inspired by our family’s vacation in Bali where we used the Gojek app for our transportation, we decided to add our brother’s nickname (he’s Goy) for recall and thus Goyjek was born!

We have several motorbikes for rent, all of which are accredited by the Land Transportation Office. We also make sure to provide essentials for a hassle-free ride around the island. Safety and convenience are key to making your tropical vacation an enjoyable experience. We take pride in being a part of the Siargao Island community. Thank you for choosing our motorcycle rental for your Siargao Island adventure. We look forward to providing you with the best experience and helping you make the most of your trip!
